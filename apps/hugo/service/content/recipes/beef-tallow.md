# 🥩 BEST FAT SOURCES

## PRIME SOURCES
✅ Brisket fat cap  
✅ Deckle fat  
✅ Beef plate rib trim  
✅ Hard white beef fat  

## OPTIONAL
✅ Suet (kidney fat)  
✅ Chuck trim  
✅ Steak trimmings  

## AVOID
❌ Excessively bloody trim  
❌ Burnt pieces  
❌ Heavy meat contamination  

---

# 🔪 PREP

## Ideal Prep
* Cut fat into small cubes/chunks
* Smaller pieces render faster
* Optional:
  * partially freeze
  * coarse grind

## Goal
Increase:
* surface area
* render efficiency
* clean extraction

---

# 🔥 RENDER METHODS

## METHOD 1 — SMOKER RENDER (Preferred)

### Why
✅ Adds smoke character  
✅ Happens during BBQ cook  
✅ Most authentic pitmaster flavor  

### Setup
* Foil pan or cast iron
* Place on smoker beside meat

### Temp
* 225–275°F

### Process
1. Add fat chunks
2. Stir occasionally
3. Allow slow render
4. Remove cracklings when browned

### Result
🔥 Smoked BBQ tallow  
🔥 Deep beef aroma  
🔥 “Pitmaster Reserve” quality  

---

## METHOD 2 — OVEN RENDER

### Temp
* 250°F

### Benefits
✅ Cleaner flavor  
✅ Easier control  
✅ Lower scorch risk  

### Best For
* neutral cooking tallow
* frying/searing

---

## METHOD 3 — STOVETOP

### Heat
* LOWEST POSSIBLE

### Risks
⚠️ Scorching  
⚠️ Burnt flavor  
⚠️ Uneven render  

### Only Use
* quick/small batches

---
