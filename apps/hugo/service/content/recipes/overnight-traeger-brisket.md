# 🥩 Overnight Traeger Brisket Runbook v1.1

## 🎯 Objectives

- Flavor: Deep wood smoke profile via early Super Smoke
- Bark: Dark mahogany crust locked in before wrapping
- Texture: Moist flat, rendered point fat, clean slices
- Effort: Maximum sleep with zero overnight intervention

---

## 🧰 Equipment & Prep

### Smoker

- Traeger Woodridge Pro
- Oak, Hickory, or Texas Blend pellets

### Tools

- Traeger meat probe
- Backup probe
- Pink butcher paper
- Cast iron pan

### Resting Setup

- Cooler
- 2–3 towels
- Aluminum foil

---

## 🔪 Trimming & Seasoning

### Brisket

- Trim into an aerodynamic oval shape
- Fat cap: 1/4 inch
- Remove hard deckle fat

### Tallow Setup

Place all trimmed fat into a cast iron pan beneath the brisket.

Benefits:

- Captures drippings
- Produces smoked beef tallow
- Creates wrap enrichment later

### Rub Blend

| Ingredient | Amount |
|------------|----------|
| Morton Coarse Kosher Salt | 9–10 tsp |
| Coarse Black Pepper | 9–10 tsp |
| Garlic Powder | 5 tsp |
| Onion Powder | 5 tsp |
| Lawry's Seasoned Salt (Optional) | 4–5 tsp |

Apply evenly across the brisket.

---

## 🔥 Smoker Layout Strategy

### Orientation

- Fat side DOWN
- Point toward back-right hot zone
- Flat toward front-left cooler zone

### Why

Protects the flat from direct pellet-grill heat.

---

# 🌙 Overnight Cooking Timeline

## Phase 1 — Smoke Loading (9:30 PM)

### Settings

- 185°F
- Super Smoke ON

### Duration

1.5–2 hours

### Target Internal Temp

95–100°F

---

## Phase 2 — Overnight Cruise (11:00 PM)

### Settings

- 225°F
- Super Smoke OFF

### Action

Go to bed.

Do not:

- Open lid
- Spritz
- Chase temperatures

---

# 🌅 Morning Evaluation & Wrap

## Morning Check (7–8 AM)

Expected temperature:

- 155–165°F

### Wrap Only When

- Bark is dark
- Bark is set
- Rub does not scrape away

---

## 🫙 Tallow Wrap

### Setup

1. Lay out two overlapping sheets of butcher paper.
2. Pour warm rendered tallow on paper.
3. Pour additional tallow over brisket.
4. Wrap tightly.

### Return To Smoker

- 250°F
- Fat side down

---

# 🏁 Finish & Rest

## Pull Criteria

### Temperature

- Usually 201–205°F

### More Important

Probe tenderness.

The probe should slide in like:

> Warm butter.

---

## Venting Step

Place wrapped brisket on counter:

- 15–20 minutes

Purpose:

- Stop carryover cooking
- Prevent mushy texture

---

## Cooler Hold

### Setup

1. Wrap brisket in foil.
2. Wrap in warm towels.
3. Place in cooler.

### Duration

Minimum:

- 2 hours

Maximum:

- 4–6 hours

---

# 🔪 Slicing

## Flat

Slice against grain:

- 1/4-inch slices

## Point

Rotate 90 degrees.

Options:

- Slice
- Burnt ends

---

# 🔥 Serving

Pour reserved warm tallow over slices immediately before serving.

Benefits:

- Moisture retention
- Rich beef flavor
- Competition-style finish
