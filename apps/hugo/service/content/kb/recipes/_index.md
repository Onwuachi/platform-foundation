# Recipes

Operational documentation for barbecue and smoked food systems.

This section treats cooking as a repeatable system, not a collection of recipes.

---

## Structure

### 🔥 Smoke Sessions
End-to-end cooking operations combining multiple proteins and timing coordination.

### 🥩 Proteins
Canonical procedures for individual meats.

### 🧰 Techniques
Reusable methods applied across multiple cooks.

### 📚 Reference
Static knowledge, charts, and constants used across all operations.

---

## Philosophy

- Cook to tenderness, not time
- Bark is validated, not assumed
- Resting is part of execution
- Every cook improves the system
