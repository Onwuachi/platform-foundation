# Smoke Sessions

Smoke Sessions document complete cooking operations from startup through serving.

Unlike the protein runbooks, these documents capture what actually happened during a cook, including observations, adjustments, timing, and lessons learned.

---

## Typical Contents

- Timeline
- Weather
- Pellet selection
- Temperature changes
- Wrapping decisions
- Rest periods
- Results
- Lessons learned

---

## Philosophy

Every cook becomes another data point that improves the canonical runbooks.

---

## System Context

Part of the Recipes knowledge base within the Onwuachi Control Plane.
