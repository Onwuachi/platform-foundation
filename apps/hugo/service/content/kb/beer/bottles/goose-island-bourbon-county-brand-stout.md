# Why I Bought It

Bourbon County Brand Stout is one of the beers that sparked my interest in
craft beer and eventually helped shape my appreciation for bourbon.

Long before I began building a bourbon collection, I looked forward to the
annual Bourbon County release and enjoyed exploring how bourbon barrels
transformed the beer.

Revisiting it today allows me to compare my current bourbon palate against the
beer that helped start the journey.
