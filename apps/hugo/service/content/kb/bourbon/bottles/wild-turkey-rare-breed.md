# About Rare Breed

Introduced in 1991, Rare Breed helped define what barrel-proof bourbon could be long before it became fashionable.

Rather than chasing extreme proof or limited allocations, Wild Turkey bottles Rare Breed at its natural barrel proof after blending mature stocks of bourbon. The result is a whiskey that delivers richness, complexity, and balance without sacrificing drinkability.

Master Distillers Jimmy Russell and Eddie Russell have kept Rare Breed remarkably consistent over the years, making it one of the safest premium bourbon purchases on the shelf.

# Comparison Notes

## Compared with Russell's Reserve Single Barrel

Rare Breed feels slightly bolder and more spice-forward.

Russell's Reserve Single Barrel offers more refinement and elegance.

Rare Breed wins on intensity.

Russell's wins on finesse.

## Compared with Old Forester 1920

1920 leans toward dark chocolate and cherry.

Rare Breed leans toward oak, caramel, baking spice, and classic Kentucky bourbon character.

## Compared with Heaven Hill Bottled in Bond

Heaven Hill BiB remains my benchmark everyday Bottled-in-Bond bourbon.

Rare Breed simply adds another level of richness, proof, mouthfeel, and complexity.
