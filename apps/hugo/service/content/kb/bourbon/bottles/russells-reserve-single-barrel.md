# Why I Bought It

After falling in love with Wild Turkey Rare Breed, I wanted to see what the Russell family's flagship single barrel bourbon could offer.

Rather than simply increasing the proof, Russell's Reserve Single Barrel focuses on barrel selection and maturity.

It represents another side of the Wild Turkey profile—more refined, more elegant, and more oak-forward while still maintaining the signature Wild Turkey character.

# About Russell's Reserve Single Barrel

Created by legendary Master Distiller **Jimmy Russell** and Master Distiller **Eddie Russell**, Russell's Reserve Single Barrel showcases individual barrels selected for exceptional quality.

Unlike Rare Breed, which blends barrels of various ages to achieve consistency, every bottle of Russell's Reserve Single Barrel comes from one carefully selected barrel.

Although each barrel varies slightly, the overall profile remains remarkably consistent thanks to Wild Turkey's strict barrel selection process.

# Appearance

Rich amber with copper highlights.

Excellent viscosity with slow, oily legs coating the glass.

Immediately looks older and richer than many bourbons at a similar price.

---

# Nose

One of the best noses in my collection.

Notes include:

- Rich vanilla
- Dark caramel
- Toasted oak
- Cherry
- Brown sugar
- Baking spices
- Orange peel
- Leather
- Light tobacco

Everything feels layered and mature without becoming overly sweet.

---

# Palate

This is where Russell's Reserve Single Barrel shines.

The first sip delivers exceptional balance between sweetness, oak, spice, and proof.

Notes I consistently find include:

- Rich caramel
- Vanilla
- Dark cherry
- Brown sugar
- Toasted oak
- Cinnamon
- Black pepper
- Leather
- Roasted nuts

Compared to Rare Breed, the spice is more restrained while the oak feels deeper and more integrated.

The mouthfeel is rich, oily, and incredibly satisfying.

---

# Finish

Long and elegant.

The caramel slowly transitions into toasted oak, baking spices, and black pepper before fading into lingering vanilla and gentle leather.

The finish lasts considerably longer than many bourbons in this price range.

---

# Overall Impression

Russell's Reserve Single Barrel is one of the finest expressions of the Wild Turkey profile.

It doesn't try to overwhelm you with proof.

Instead, it delivers maturity, complexity, balance, and remarkable barrel character.

Every pour feels intentional.

This is the bottle I reach for when I want something that feels a little more refined than Rare Breed without losing what makes Wild Turkey special.

# Final Thoughts

Russell's Reserve Single Barrel represents everything I enjoy about bourbon.

It has mature oak, rich caramel, balanced spice, outstanding mouthfeel, and enough proof to remain exciting without becoming overpowering.

As my palate has evolved, I've realized I appreciate refinement just as much as intensity.

Rare Breed may still edge it out as my favorite overall bottle because of its unbeatable value and barrel-proof character, but Russell's Reserve Single Barrel is arguably the more polished bourbon.

It's one of the bottles that has helped define my palate and has unquestionably earned its place in my Top 5.
